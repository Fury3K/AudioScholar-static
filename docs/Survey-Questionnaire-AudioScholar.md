## **Survey Instructions**

Thank you for participating in the evaluation of **AudioScholar**, an educational application designed to automate lecture note-taking using AI.

*   **Purpose:** To evaluate the usability, technical quality, and usefulness of the AudioScholar application.
*   **Time Required:** Approximately 10-15 minutes.
*   **Confidentiality:** All responses are anonymous and will be used solely for academic research and system improvement.
*   **Instructions:** Please answer all questions honestly based on your experience testing the application. There are no right or wrong answers.
